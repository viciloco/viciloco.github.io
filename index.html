    About ViciLoco

  • The pronunciation of "ViciLoco" can be broken down as follows:
  • - Vici: Pronounced as "vee-sigh" (rhymes with "see-die" or "sky").
  • - Loco: Pronounced as "loh-koh" (rhymes with "so-so" or "no-go").
  • So, when pronounced together, it would be "vee-sigh-loh-koh."

    • "ViciLoco" is a made-up name, and as such, it does not have a specific meaning in any particular language. It sounded like a pretty good name for a location-based social media app. The name combines elements of "Vici" (derived from the Latin word "vici," meaning "I conquered" or "I won") and "Loco" (a Spanish word meaning "crazy" or "mad"). The combination of these elements aims to evoke a sense of excitement, exploration, and connection in a location-based context. And the great part about the words "vici" and "loco" is that they really fit in the concept, with "vici" being a substring of "vicinity" and "loco" being similar to "local". We think we've made it clear that this name is pretty perfect.


  • ViciLoco is a simple and easy method to connect with the people around you. Anywhere, anytime.
    We aim to be the service that connects people with others in their localities, and ViciLoco travels with you when you're on vacation too, allowing you to feel at home even if you're thousands of miles away.
