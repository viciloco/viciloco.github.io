Frequently Asked Questions


How do I create an account?

To create an account on ViciLoco, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage.
  2. Provide the required information, such as your name, email address, and password.
  3. Agree to the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.
  4. Click on the "Sign Up" button to complete the registration process.
Can I use ViciLoco on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use ViciLoco on multiple devices. Simply log in to your account from the device you wish to use, and you'll have access to your account and all its features.

How can I reset my password?

If you need to reset your password, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the ViciLoco login page.
  2. Click on the "Forgot Password?" link.
  3. Enter the email address associated with your ViciLoco account.
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
Does ViciLoco have any age restrictions?

Yes, ViciLoco has age restrictions to keep everyone safe.

As outlined in the Terms of Use, ViciLoco is 18+ or 13+ with parental consent.

Is there a ViciLoco app?

No, ViciLoco does not have an official app, but the website is mobile-friendly and will work fine on your phone. Any apps posing as ViciLoco are not affiliated with ViciLoco and should be reported to the app store and ViciLoco immediately at contact@guessoword.com. Thank you.
